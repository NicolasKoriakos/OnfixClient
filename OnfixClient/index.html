FAQs

Find answers to common questions about onfix and how it works.

How does onfix work?

onfix works by connecting users with specialists who can help them fix issues with their machines. Users can schedule a meeting with a specialist and discuss their problem to find a solution.

Is onfix available worldwide?

Yes, onfix is available worldwide. We have a network of specialists from different regions who can assist users with their machine issues regardless of their location.

How can I schedule a meeting?

To schedule a meeting, simply sign up for an onfix account and browse through our list of available specialists. Once you find a specialist that suits your needs, you can select a convenient time slot and book the meeting.

Is onfix secure?

Yes, onfix takes security seriously. We ensure that all communication between users and specialists is encrypted and stored securely. Additionally, we carefully vet our specialists to ensure they are qualified and trustworthy.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We strive to provide the best service possible, but if you're not satisfied with your meeting, please let us know. We have a satisfaction guarantee and will work with you to address any concerns or issues you may have.
